Miao Liu is a computational materials scientist and bass fish angler

My research interests revolve around materials design & physics in nanoscale. I attended University of Utah, graduating with a Ph.D in Materials Science and Engineering. Afterwards, I joined Lawrence Berkeley National Lab as a postdoc fellow, working on beyond Lithium energy storage from an In silico, material genome approach.
